# Guideline Source Strategy

## Priority Order

1. Thai FDA, NDP, NDI, NLEM, Thai RDU.
2. Thai MOPH and Department of Medical Services.
3. Thai specialty society guidelines.
4. Thai Pediatric Society and pediatric subspecialty CPGs.
5. WHO, CDC, NICE, IDSA, GINA, GOLD, ACG, AAP.
6. BNFC, Lexicomp Pediatric, Harriet Lane, Micromedex, drug monographs.
7. Product label or SmPC.
8. Local clinic preference only.

## Evidence Requirements

Every active clinical rule must have source ID, document title, version/date, URL or local path, page/section, extracted statement summary, reviewer, and review date.

## Unsupported Data

If a dose, indication, contraindication, safety rule, duration, pediatric threshold, or price cannot be traced to a source, leave the field blank and keep `manual_review: true`.

## Current Status

No local guideline files were present during this build. The source registry and gap list are therefore pending source access, and clinical source-linked rules remain inactive.
